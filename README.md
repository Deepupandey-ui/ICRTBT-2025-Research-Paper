## 📘 About The Research

This research paper, **“Biofuel – Powering a Sustainable Future”**, was published in **ICRTBT-2025 (International Conference on Recent Trends in Biosciences and Technology)** organized by **Galgotias University**.

The research focuses on solving real-world environmental challenges using sustainable biofuel systems, biomass utilization, and renewable energy technologies.

---

## 🎯 Research Objectives

- 🌾 Smart Biomass Mapping using digital geospatial tools
- ⚡ Sustainable Ethanol Production from agricultural waste
- 🔬 Fermentation & Process Optimization techniques
- 🚜 Alternatives to Stubble Burning using modern agricultural methods
- 🌍 Environmental & Socio-Economic Impact Analysis

---

## 💡 Problem Statement

India generates massive agricultural biomass waste every year, leading to:

- Air pollution from stubble burning
- Loss of renewable energy potential
- Environmental degradation
- Economic inefficiencies for farmers

This research explores scalable and sustainable solutions for converting biomass waste into useful energy resources.
